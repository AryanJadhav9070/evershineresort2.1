Welcome to Evershine

A Sanctuary of
Luxury & Serenity

Perched at 4,500 feet above sea level, Evershine Resort & Spa is a majestic retreat inspired by the timeless grandeur of Rajasthani palaces. Here, royal elegance meets the pristine beauty of the Western Ghats.

Our commitment to exceptional hospitality ensures every moment of your stay is crafted with care, from the warm welcome at arrival to the personalized experiences that await throughout your journey.
